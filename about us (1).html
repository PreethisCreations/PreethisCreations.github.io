About Us


Welcome to Preethi’s Creations, your one stop shop for handicraft and home décor items. It is our effort to bring you unique selection of traditional art and craft which are converted into a range of attractive contemporary products.

Preethi’s Creations is about bringing the world a little closer together. We try and bring you the best designs from artisans and craftsmen from all over the world, and deliver them at exceptional value to our customers.

Founded in 2015, the company has come a long way from its humble beginnings as a store operating from home to this booming online store, all because of company’s focus on three main ideologies: best customer service, high quality craftsmanship and uniqueness of the product.

We aspire to provide the best experience with best range of products at a fair price to all our clients.

Looking forward to welcoming you to our family of proud handicraft owners!

Covid update: Currently we are presenting handicrafts and home decor items only from India due to logistical reasons. We are hoping to bring you beautiful products from other parts of the world as soon as the current situation eases. We count on your understanding.
